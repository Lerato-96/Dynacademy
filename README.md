# Dynacademy – AI-Powered Education & Career Guidance Platform

## Overview
Dynacademy is an AI-powered education and career guidance platform designed to support South African learners with academic planning, career guidance, university matching, bursary recommendations, and AI-powered study assistance.

The platform combines Artificial Intelligence, educational support systems, data analytics, and responsive dashboard technologies into one centralized digital solution.

Dynacademy aims to improve educational accessibility, learner support, career readiness, and digital transformation within the education sector.
